What are Machinies?

Machinies are a collection of 8,888 units of machine forever run on Ethereum Blockchain. Which acts as your representative of being active for 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Owning Machinies in your wallet does not only to keep you on the move but grants ownership privilege.

There is no single unit that is the same, each Machinie is algorithmically generated from hand-made molds. Differences in each Machinie are mold, background, monitor count, and type.
